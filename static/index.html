Aston Hack 2017

11th & 12th November


    We are inviting student hackers to join us for a frenzied 24 hours of designing, coding and making. Whether this will be your first hackathon or your 20th come join us and build something amazing!






Not sure what a hackathon is?

A hackathon is where teams of up to 4 people work together to build something over 24 hours. Sponsors present themes for which prizes will be awarded and at the end of the 24 hours where teams present their work to the judges.

FAQs

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an invention marathon. Students come together to build cool software & hardware hacks over 24-48 hours. It’s very beginner friendly.

How much does it cost?

Nothing! Attending a hackathon is free! 😁

Do I need to be a student to attend?

Yes. Only students over the age of 18 who are currently enrolled at a college/university or have graduated within the past 12 months are eligible to attend as hackers.


Where/when is the event?

Check in will be between 10am and 11am in the main foyer of Aston University, Aston Triangle, Birmingham, B4 7ET. Don’t worry, we’ll have signs so you don’t get lost in the labyrinth that is Aston

What is Major League Hacking?

Major League Hacking is the official student hackathon league in North America & Europe. They work with over 200 Member Events and empower over 70,000 students every year.

Is there a code of conduct?

Yes, we stick strongly to the MLH code of conduct. You can find it here.

Yes! We’d love for your company to sponsor, and help make this fantastic event possible! You can view our sponsorship document here. We would ask all potential sponsors to email hello@astonhack.co.uk and one of the team will get back to you.
